1 can cream of potato soup
1 can creamed corn
Rotisserie chicken (or 2 cans of canned chicken)
2 cans evaporated milk (optionally substitute half & half)
1 cube chicken bouillon, crushed (or equivalent Better Than Bouillon)
1 T. each minced onion and garlic

Optional add’l veg:
Cans of carrots, potatoes, and corn

In a large sauce pan, sauté minced onion and garlic in butter until translucent. Combine all ingredients and stir until blended. Bring to boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally, then reduce to simmer.
