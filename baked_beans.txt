Baked Beans
----
2 16 oz cans pork and beans
4 slices bacon
.5c minced onion
2 cloves garlic
2T brown sugar
1T worcestershire sauce
1t yellow mustard

Cook bacon till crisp, then cook onion and garlic in drippings until tender. Add crumbled bacon and onion and garlic to pork and beans. Stir in sugar, sauce and mustard. Cook at 325 for 1.5 - 1.75 hrs. (Optionally: cook in microwave for 10-15 minutes then finish in oven; takes much less time.)
