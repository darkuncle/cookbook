Baked Beans
----
2 16 oz cans pork and beans
4 slices bacon
.5c minced onion
2T brown sugar
1T worcestershire sauce
1t yellow mustard

Cook bacon till crisp, then cook onion in drippings until tender. Add crumbled
bacon and onion to pork and beans. Stir in sugar, sauce and mustard. Cook at
325 for 1.5 - 1.75 hrs. (Optionally: cook in microwave for 10-15 minutes then
finish in oven; takes much less time.)
