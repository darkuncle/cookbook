Franks and Beans Casserole

3 T. brown sugar
1 t. dry mustard
21 oz. can pork and beans
15 oz. can chili (no beans)
1 lb. hot dogs, sliced
3 oz. can French fried onions

Topping:
1 c. flour
1.25 c. mashed potato flakes
1.5 t. baking powder
0.5 t. salt
0.75 c. milk
2 T. butter, melted
8.5 oz. can (1 c.) creamed corn
1 egg
1 T. dried parsley

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Combine all ingredients (except Topping) in an
ungreased 3 qt. casserole or 9"x13" baking dish.

Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup; level off. Combine all Topping
ingredients except parsley flakes; blend well. Drop by spoonfuls around edge of
casserole; sprinkle with parsley flakes. Bake at 400 degrees for 35-40 minutes
or until Topping is light golden brown and meat mixture is bubbly.
Makes 6-8 servings.

NOTE: if using self-rising flour, omit baking powder and salt. For high altitude
cooking, add 3 T. water to meat and bean mixture.
