Blueberry Buckle
================
.5c butter
.75c sugar
1 egg
2c sifted flour
2.5t baking powder
.25t salt
.5c milk
2c fresh blueberries (frozen will do)

topping
--------
.5c sugar
.5c flour
.5t cinnamon
.25c butter

cream butter/sugar, add egg and beat till fluffy. Sift dry ingredients
together. Add to creamed mixture alternately with milk. Mix in berries.
Spread in 11x7 pan; 8x8 will do (double recipe for 13x9). Mix dry topping
ingredients and cut in butter w/pastry blender till crumbly; scatter evenly over berries. Bake @ 350F for 45 min. Serve warm.
