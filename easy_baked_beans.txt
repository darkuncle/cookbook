Easy Baked Beans

4 slices bacon
1/2 c. chopped onion
2 16 oz. cans pork & beans
2 T. brown sugar
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1 t. yellow mustard

Preheat oven to 325 F.

Cook bacon till crisp. Drain, reserving 2 tablespoons drippings. Crumble bacon.

Cook onion in drippings till tender, then combine all ingredients in a 1.5 qt. casserole dish and mix well.

Bake uncovered at 325 F. for 1.5 to 2 hours. Serves 6.
