Tanzanian Coconut Rice

2 c. basmati rice
1.5 c. water
1 c. canned, unsweetened coconut milk
0.5 c. each peas & diced carrots
1 t. turmeric powder (can substitute 0.5 t. each of curry powder and ginger)
salt to taste

Rinse and drain rice, then add to a saucepan with all of the above ingredients
and bring to a boil. Cover and reduce heat to low, then cook about 15 more
minutes or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat and let
stand 5 minutes, covered. Fluff with fork; serves 6-8.
