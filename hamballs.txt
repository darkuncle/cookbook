2.5 lbs. ground ham
2 lbs. lean ground pork
1 lb. lean ground beef
3 c. graham cracker crumbs (approx. 2 packages out of a box)
3 beaten eggs
2 c. milk

Shape into 25-35 tangerine-sized balls. Mix all sauce ingredients (below),
pour over balls and bake at 325 F. for 1.5 hours. Serve over rice.

Freeze uncooked hamballs and sauce separately for up to six months.

Sauce:
2 cans tomato soup
.75 c. vinegar
2.25 c. packed brown sugar
2 t. dry mustard
