Cheeseburger Chowder
--------------------
2 lb. ground beef
1 c celery, finely chopped
1 c onion, chopped
2 T minced garlic
6 T flour
1 t salt
8 c milk
2 T beef boullion granules (or 2 t Better Than Boullion)
8 oz. Velveeta, cubed
4 oz. shredded Swiss/Gruyere blend

Brown beef in large skillet and drain. add celery, garlic, and onion and cook until
tender. Blend in flour and salt, then stir in milk and boullion. Cook and
stir over low heat till thick and bubbly. Add cheese and stir until cheese
is completely melted. 350 calories per serving; serves 8.
