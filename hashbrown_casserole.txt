Hashbrown Casserole
===================

30 oz. bag shredded potatoes
2 c. sour cream
2 c. sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 c. sweet onion, chopped
1/4 c. butter, melted
1 10 oz. can cream of chicken soup
salt and pepper to taste

Mix all ingredients and pour into a 9"x13" pan. Top with a little extra cheese
and bake at 350F for 30 minutes, or until exposed potatoes are golden and crispy.
