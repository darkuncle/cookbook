Strawberry Pretzel Salad

INGREDIENTS
 * 2 c. crushed pretzels
 * 1/4 c. white sugar
 * 1 T. brown sugar
 * 3/4 cup butter, melted
 * 1 cup white sugar
 * 1/2 t. vanilla
 * 2 (8 oz.) packages cream cheese
 * 1 (8 oz.) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
 * 2 (3 oz.) packages strawberry flavored gelatin
 * 1 1/4 cup boiling water
 * 1 (24 oz.) carton frozen sliced sweetened strawberries, thawed

DIRECTIONS
 1. In a medium bowl, stir together the gelatin mix and boiling water. Mix in 
strawberries and juice, and chill until slightly thickened (~ 1 hour). 
 2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix together the pretzels, sugar, and melted butter. 
Press into the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan. Bake for 10 minutes (do not overbake). Cool 
completely.
 3. In a medium bowl, beat 1 c. sugar, vanilla, and cream cheese until smooth. Fold in 
whipped topping. Spread evenly over the cooled crust. Refrigerate until set, about 30 
minutes. 
 4. Pour thickened gelatin mixture over cream cheese mixture in pan. Refrigerate until 
completely chilled, at least 1 hour.
